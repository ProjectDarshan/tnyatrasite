About the Ashram

Our ashram work began through volunteer service for temple visitors and has grown into a structured support center for yatras, retreats, and elder well-being. We keep a simple approach: clarity, compassion, and disciplined service.

Our way of life

Daily prayer, shared meals, and seva are central to our routine. We combine devotional practice with practical support for pilgrims and families.

Open doors

Visitors come for guidance, rest, and connection. Elders receive attentive care, while travelers get route planning and culturally respectful support.

Pilgrimage & temple tours

The yatra is not sightseeing—it is darśan, the blessed seeing. We plan routes around festival calendars, monsoon light, and the old stone corridors where bare feet have polished the floor for centuries.

  • Curated circuits — Chola heartland, Pandya shrines, coastal sthalams, and hill temples reached by gentle climbs with rest stops.
  • Small groups — Intentional numbers so guides can explain iconography, local lore, and the meaning of each mandapam without haste.
  • Care on the path — Clean drinking water, modest footwear advice, first-aid readiness, and respect for each pilgrim's pace.
  • Evening satsang — After the day's steps, we gather for tea, Tamil devotional verse, and quiet reflection under open sky.
பக்தியோடு கால் வைத்தால் ஒவ்வொரு படியும் பூஜையே. With devotion in each step, every footfall is its own offering—words we remember when the road grows long.

Services

Our services are built for practical comfort, spiritual grounding, and dependable travel support.

Old age home

Comfortable accommodation, regular meals, routine health check support, and devotional activities for seniors in a dignified setting.

Spiritual retreats

Quiet stay programs with prayer, reflection, guided silence, and optional temple visits designed for varied energy levels.

Tour guiding

Guided temple itineraries for families and groups with local knowledge, temple etiquette guidance, and bilingual support.

Events & yatra calendar

Dates shift with the lunar calendar; below is a sample rhythm—confirm before you travel.

Season Gathering Notes
January Pongal seva & village annadanam Volunteer slots open two months prior.
March–April Chithirai festival support yatra Limited seats; early registration.
June–July Monsoon retreat (5 nights) Indoor sessions if rains are heavy.
September Navaratri garland-making & nightly arati Family-friendly; all ages welcome.
November–December Karthigai deepam hill circuit Moderate walking; torches provided.
